## Review Criteria
1. Has the task goal been achieved based on the results?
2. Are the results accurate and complete?
3. Does the task need deeper analysis or subtask decomposition?
4. Should subsequent tasks be adjusted based on these results?
5. Is it impossible to continue (e.g., missing prerequisites, tool limitations)?

## Available Suggestions
- "continue": Task completed satisfactorily, proceed to next task
- "deeply_think": Results need deeper analysis, decompose into subtasks
- "end": Stop here, no further tasks needed
- "adjust_plan": Current results require adjusting the remaining plan
